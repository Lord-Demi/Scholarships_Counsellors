Exploring Scholarships: Unveiling the New Switzerland University Scholar Honors

Embarking on your academic journey at ETH Zurich is not just a step, but a transformative leap into a world of cutting-edge possibilities. For outstanding transfer students and prospective international freshmen, the ETH Scholar Expedition embodies your potential, offering more than just financial support – it provides an immersive environment dedicated to knowledge, innovation, and addressing the world's most complex challenges.

Beyond Financial Aid: An Empowering Odyssey

Imagine an environment where your pursuit of academic excellence is met with unwavering encouragement. The ETH Scholar Program transcends traditional scholarships. It's a meticulously crafted path intended to inspire, empower, and propel you toward a future marked by groundbreaking contributions. Guided by financial support, you'll navigate an atmosphere that values learning, research, and the exchange of ideas, equipping you with the tools to tackle society's most intricate challenges.

Seamless Integration: Your ETH Adventure Begins

From the moment you set foot on ETH Zurich's illustrious campus, you're already on the trajectory towards achieving the ETH Scholar Expedition. As a registered student, you're automatically considered for this esteemed recognition. No separate application process is required. However, to ensure consideration, make sure the university's International Admissions Office receives all your application materials – application submission, official transcripts, test scores, financial documentation, and application fee – by the scholarship priority date of April 1.

Eligibility Clarified

The ETH Scholar Program extends its embrace to individuals who meet specific criteria thoughtfully designed to encompass dedication and promise.

  • Global Vision: ETH Zurich is a dynamic global community, ready to welcome international scholars as they shape their academic journeys.
  • Demonstrated Financial Need: Financial circumstances will not hinder your progress; they will propel it forward.
  • Academic Excellence: Your academic journey evolves into a tapestry of accomplishments.
  • Language Proficiency: Proficiency in English communication ensures your ideas resonate on an international scale.
  • Innovative Achievement: Remarkable test scores and academic performance underscore your commitment to pioneering growth.
  • Student Visa Advantage: Student visa holders are uniquely poised to benefit from this exceptional program.
  • Exclusion for Sponsored Students: While sponsored students are valued, they are not eligible for this specific honor.

A Journey of Continued Opportunities

Your narrative is poised to unfold, and the ETH Scholar Expedition is the blueprint that amplifies your story. Beyond scholarships, these distinctions pave the way for an enriching academic journey – one that challenges, inspires, and shapes the future you've always envisioned.
