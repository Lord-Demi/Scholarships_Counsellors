Tongarewa Scholarship: Empowering Global Excellence at Victoria University of Wellington

Unlock a world of opportunities and showcase your brilliance with the Tongarewa Scholarship, a prestigious initiative by the Victoria University of Wellington. This scholarship reflects the university's deep commitment to its international student community, offering a platform for exceptional individuals like you to excel in your academic pursuits.

Embrace Your Academic Journey

Whether you're about to embark on your undergraduate adventure or are ready to conquer the realm of postgraduate studies, the Tongarewa Scholarship awaits your arrival. Through this scholarship, you become part of a dynamic network of global thinkers, innovators, and changemakers, setting the stage for a transformative educational experience.

Your Gateway to the Future

As a Tongarewa Scholarship recipient, you're not just a student; you're an Ambassador for Victoria University of Wellington. You'll be invited to participate in events and marketing initiatives, sharing your unique journey and enriching the university community.

Fields of Exploration

The scholarship casts a wide net, encompassing Bachelor's degrees, Master's by Research degrees, or Master's by Coursework degrees in any subject. Your passion and aspirations find a home within the halls of Victoria University of Wellington.

Illuminate Your Path

With up to 10 scholarships of NZ$10,000 for Bachelors and Masters degrees, and up to 9 scholarships of NZ$5,000 for Masters degrees, the Tongarewa Scholarship is a beacon of financial support. This partial fee-based scholarship is dedicated to nurturing your potential, contributing to your tuition fees for one year of study.

An Invitation to Reside

The journey doesn't stop at academics. Tongarewa Scholarship recipients are offered guaranteed accommodation in the Halls of Residence. Secure your place by submitting your accommodation application to the Accommodation Service by the stipulated deadline.

The Quest for Eligibility

Are you ready to take the next step? The Tongarewa Scholarship is open to international students from across the globe. To be eligible, you must be commencing your first year of an undergraduate degree or entering a postgraduate degree program. An offer of place from Victoria University of Wellington, whether conditional or unconditional, is your passport to eligibility.

Illuminate Your Path

Eager to embrace this unparalleled opportunity? Mark your calendar and submit your completed online application before the deadlines: 1 June 2023 (Trimester 2, 2023), 1 September 2023 (Trimester 3, 2023), or 30 November 2023 (Trimester 1, 2024).
