Seamless Integration: Your Erasmus Adventure Begins

From the moment you set foot on Erasmus University Rotterdam's esteemed campus, you're already on the trajectory towards achieving the Erasmus Scholar Pursuit. As a registered student, you're automatically considered for this prestigious recognition. No separate application process is required. However, to ensure consideration, make sure the University's International Admissions Office receives all your application materials – application submission, official transcripts, test scores, financial documentation, and application fee – by the scholarship priority date of April 1.
